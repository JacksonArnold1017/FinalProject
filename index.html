Robert Kirkman

This page is dedicated to comic book legend Robert Kirkman. The creator of many stories such as Invincible, The Walking Dead, and Marvel Zombies.

His stories have inspired me and countless others to write our own stories. This website was made to show our gratitude, and to introduce many future fans to his work.

Kirkman's stories are dark, unforgiving, and very entertaining.Most of his work being turned into shows on television, on channels like AMC.

This page will explore the many different stories he's created and how they still are talked about and used years later.
